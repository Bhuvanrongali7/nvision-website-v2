GSA Multiple Award Schedule (MAS)
Contract No: GS-35F-414DA

The GSA IT MAS is the government's primary source for IT acquisitions — recognized for cost savings, time savings, and built-in compliance value. An IDIQ contract under GSA's Federal Acquisition Service, offering a comprehensive array of IT products, services, and solutions.

Agencies can procure NVision IT's Oracle ERP, BI, cloud, custom development, and staffing services directly through pre-negotiated pricing — no competitive RFP required.
